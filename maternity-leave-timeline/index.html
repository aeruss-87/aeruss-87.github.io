How programs sequence (STD, then PFL)

The key insight: Ryder STD and CA PFL cover different phases of your leave and run back-to-back, not at the same time. This means they can add up to significant coverage.

Phase 1: Disability (recovery after delivery)
Ryder STD is your primary income source. CA SDI also covers this phase, but the two offset each other. You receive the higher of the two, not both. File for both as a safety net in case STD is denied.
CA SDI duration: up to 52 weeks per claim. For pregnancy, SDI typically covers up to 4 weeks before delivery + 6 weeks (vaginal) or 8 weeks (C-section) after. Your doctor certifies the exact period.

Phase 2: Bonding (after disability ends)
CA PFL kicks in after disability ends. Ryder STD does not cover bonding. SF PPLO supplements PFL if you're in San Francisco.

Total income replacement: Up to 12 weeks of Ryder STD during disability, then up to 8 weeks of CA PFL during bonding = up to 20 weeks of some level of pay. In practice, the disability phase is typically 6-8 weeks, so most employees see 14-16 weeks of income replacement total.

For 12+ month employees: Baton tops up your total to 100% of salary for 12 weeks across both phases.

For under-12-month employees: You receive STD at 80% during disability, then PFL at ~70% during bonding. There is no Baton top-up.

Action steps

As early as possible: Tell your manager. Loop in Laura Forte or Ashley Russell.

6+ weeks before leave: File leave request on MyRyder. Contact Lincoln Financial. Start CA EDD application. All three tracks run in parallel.

2-3 weeks before leave: Get healthcare provider certifications. Upload to MyRyder and Lincoln. Submit CA SDI claim.

After birth: File CA PFL bonding claim through EDD. Notify Lincoln of delivery date and type.

Before returning: Contact Ryder and Lincoln 1-2 weeks before return. Get doctor's clearance note. Drug screen required if out 90+ days.
The biggest trap: FMLA approval does not mean you get paid. FMLA is job protection only. You must separately file for STD and SDI to receive income during leave. File all three tracks as early as you can.
