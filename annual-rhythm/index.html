January trigger: Start Q1 Performance Prep

Around January 19 (week 3), begin building templates, manager guide, and training materials. You have about two weeks before the February kickoff. Get dates approved with Grant before the end of January.

June trigger: Start Q3 Performance Prep

Around June 8 (week 2), begin the 6-week prep window for the July cycle. This time overlaps with Q3 OKR prep and the June OKR review — start promptly so materials are ready before OKR week in July.

Two busy overlap periods to plan for

Late June through mid-July: Q3 OKR Review, Q3 OKR Setting (OKR Week), and Q3 performance prep all converge. Performance materials must be finalized before OKR week begins or you will be building the cycle while running it.

Late March: Q1 performance results delivery overlaps with Q2 OKR Review. Managers are delivering performance conversations at the same time as OKR retrospectives are running.
