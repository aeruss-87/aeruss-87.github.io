2. The EA Mindset & Prompting

Move from one-off questions to Data Synthesis. Use Gemini to reason across disparate systems.

The C.R.A.F.T. Framework

  • C Context: Give Gemini the background.
  • R Role: Assign a persona (e.g., "You are an expert Chief of Staff").
  • A Action: State the specific task.
  • F Format: Specify the output (e.g., table, email draft).
  • T Target: Define the audience or specific constraints.
Pro Tip: Use "Canvas Mode" in Gemini Enterprise for iterative editing and better control over long-form content.

3. Gemini Gems: Your AI Specialized Team

Gems are custom versions of Gemini with "locked in" instructions and knowledge.

The Ghostwriter
Mimics your Executive's voice for drafts, memos, and posts.
The Logistics Architect
Focuses on optimization, buffer times, and "plan B" travel thinking.
The Briefing Partner
Scans attendee lists to provide bios and suggested icebreakers.

4. High-Impact Use Cases & Prompts

Multi-Tab Synthesis (Gemini in Chrome)

Pin tabs (e.g., Expense/Travel System, Gmail, Calendar) and use these prompts:

"Analyze my pinned travel confirmations and the session invites on [Name] calendar. Create a new doc with a comprehensive travel itinerary for [City name] including flight times, hotel address, and a chronological list of the confirmed sessions. Flag any overlapping events."
"Create calendar invites for the flights and hotel on [Executive Name] calendar."

Intelligent Availability Search

Visible Tab: Your executive's calendar.

"Please list all available 30-minute windows for a meeting this week. I'd like you to consider all focus time blocks and all meetings color coded in Flamingo (if applicable)."

Offsite Ideation & Planning

"My team of 12 needs a three-day off-site. We have a moderate budget. Generate three locations and some scrappy fun event ideas, along with thematic concepts for each that align with our team's goals."
"I'm thinking about October for these locations, is there anything I should consider?"

Summit Session Management

"Please create calendar invites for these sessions based on the open agenda tab."
