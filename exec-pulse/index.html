What's coming to Exec Pulse

This is an MVP built for the EA Rise AI Hackathon. The features below are planned for upcoming releases. Have a request? Send it to ashley@baton.io.

Smarter Calendar

Coming soon
Back-to-Back Flag
Automatically detect days with 3 or more consecutive meetings and surface them as a focus risk.
Coming soon
Meeting Load Score
A daily focus-time score showing how much unblocked thinking time your exec has each day.
Planned
New Face Alert
Flag first-time attendees your exec hasn't met before so you can prep context or a brief bio.

Deeper Email Intelligence

Coming soon
VIP Inbox Filter
Define a list of VIP senders. Their threads always surface first, regardless of thread age.
Planned
Pending Approvals
Detect emails explicitly requesting a decision, approval, or sign-off from your exec.
Planned
Decision Backlog
Track decisions your exec has been asked to make that haven't received a clear yes/no yet.

Meeting Prep

Planned
Auto Prep Brief
For meetings flagged as Needs Prep, generate a one-paragraph context brief from prior meeting notes and email threads.
Planned
Action Item Extraction
Pull open action items assigned to your exec from recent meeting transcripts (Fathom, Granola, Otter).

EA Workload

Planned
Delegation Tracker
Track tasks your exec delegated and flag ones with no follow-up confirmation after 5+ days.
Planned
EA To-Dos Tab
A personal section just for the EA: outstanding tasks, pending logistics, and things to confirm before week's end.

Built by Ashley Russell, People Ops at Baton · This is an MVP. Feedback shapes what ships next.
